Tsubomi Hanasaki is a timid girl in the second year of middle school who adores flowers. Tsubomi and family are returning to their hometown when she experiences a weird dream with a large withering tree and a fairy fleeing the scene. The fairy that Tsubomi dreamt about confronts her a few days later and pleads for her to become the next legendary warrior Pretty Cure and to restore the wilting 'Heart Tree'. Tsubomi is forced to accept when a Desert Apostle strikes and steal the Heart Flower of
Erika Kurumi, Tsubomi's new classmate.
To retrieve Erika's heartflower she transforms
into Cure Blossom and fights.
